Rare Privacy-Designed Bathroom

Unlike most units in Lahaina Shores, our bathroom features solid wood doors separating the toilet and shower from the vanity area. Perfect for families, friends, or colleagues traveling together. One person can shower while another uses the sink and vanity - no awkward moments or waiting around. While other units removed walls for an open feel, we kept the functionality that matters most.

Welcome Home

Our King's View (Mauka & Rainbows) unit at Lahaina Shores Beach Resort offers stunning mountain and rainbow views with delightful surprises - spot the iconic banyan tree to the left and catch glimpses of Lahaina Harbor from your lanai.

Sleeps up to 4 guests comfortably: Queen bed plus two Serta convertible twin beds - perfect for families or friends traveling together.

The space reflects our family's Lahaina roots, with carefully curated pieces from The Whaler Ltd., the beloved Front Street shop that was part of historic Lahaina's fabric. Every design choice honors that legacy while providing modern comfort.
